A Full Stack Developer is a versatile tech professional capable of handling both the "front-end" and "back-end" of a website or application. The front-end involves everything users interact with directly, like layout and buttons, using languages like HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. The back-end focuses on the server, database, and internal logic that powers the app, often using tools like Node.js, Python, or SQL. Because they understand the entire "stack" of technology, these developers can build a complete project from scratch, bridge communication between different teams, and troubleshoot issues across the entire development process efficiently.

A Full Stack Developer is an "all-rounder" in web development. They master the front-end (what you see) and the back-end (how it works). By managing databases, servers, and user interfaces, they can build entire applications solo or lead teams by understanding how every piece of the digital puzzle fits together.
